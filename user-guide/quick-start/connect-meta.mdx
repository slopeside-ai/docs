## Connecting Meta Ads

Slopeside delivers audiences directly into Meta Ads. To do this, your Meta Ads account must be connected with the correct permissions.

Most setup issues happen here, so it’s worth taking a minute to confirm access before moving forward.

---

## What gets connected

When you connect Meta Ads, you allow Slopeside to:
- Deliver audiences into your ad account
- Keep those audiences updated
- Make them available for campaign use

Slopeside does not create campaigns, ads, or budgets.

---

## Required permissions

The Meta user completing this step must:
- Have admin access to the ad account
- Have permission to manage custom audiences
- Be connected through the correct Business Manager

If permissions are missing, the connection may appear successful but fail later during activation.

---

## How to connect

1. Go to the Integrations or Settings area in Slopeside  
2. Select **Connect Meta Ads**  
3. Log in with the correct Meta user  
4. Confirm the correct ad account during authorization  

Once connected, you’ll see the connection status in your dashboard.

---

## Common questions

**Can I reconnect later?**  
Yes. If permissions change in Meta, you can reconnect at any time.

**Does this affect live campaigns?**  
No. Connecting Meta Ads does not change existing campaigns.
