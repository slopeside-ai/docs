description: "How to link your Meta Ads account to Slopeside so audiences can be delivered."
---

Connecting your Meta account is the first step. Until it is connected, audiences cannot be delivered or activated.

## Before you start

Make sure you have:

- Admin access to your Meta Business Portfolio (formerly Business Manager)

- Access to the specific Ad Account you want to use with Slopeside

- Your Meta account is not restricted or under review

<Note>

If your Meta account is managed by an agency, you will need either admin access yourself or your agency will need to complete this step on your behalf. Agencies can connect client accounts through their own Slopeside dashboard.

</Note>

## How to connect

<Steps>

  <Step title="Open Settings">

      In your Slopeside dashboard, go to Settings then Connected Accounts.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Connect Meta">

      Select Connect Meta Ads Account. This opens the Leadsie authorization flow.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize access">

      Log in to Meta and grant Slopeside access to your Ad Account. You will be asked to approve the following permissions:

          - View your ad account performance and settings

          - Manage your custom audiences

      Both permissions are required. Slopeside uses view access to pull performance data for the Performance tab, and manage access to deliver audiences to your account.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your Ad Account">

      If you have multiple ad accounts in your Business Portfolio, select the specific account you want to use with Slopeside.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm connection">

      Return to the Slopeside dashboard. Your account status should show Connected with a green indicator.

  </Step>

</Steps>

## What happens after you connect

- Your Meta account is verified and linked to your Slopeside workspace

- Audiences you activate will be delivered to the connected ad account within 24 hours

- The Performance tab will be able to pull ad set-level data once you tag your test ad sets

## Troubleshooting

If the connection fails or shows as disconnected after completing the flow, see [Meta Connection Troubleshooting](/user-guide/troubleshoot/meta-connection).

Common causes include:

- Insufficient permissions in your Meta Business Portfolio

- The ad account not being assigned to your Business Portfolio

- A Meta session timeout during the authorization flow (try again)
