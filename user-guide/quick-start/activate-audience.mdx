## Activating your first audience

Activating an audience means making it available inside your Meta Ads account.

Once activated, the audience can be used directly in campaigns or as a seed for expansion and lookalikes.

---

## What activation does

When you activate an audience:
- Slopeside delivers it into Meta Ads
- Meta begins recognizing it as an available audience
- The audience stays updated based on refresh behavior

Activation does not launch ads or spend budget on its own.

---

## What to expect after activation

After activation:
- The audience will appear in Meta Ads
- You can select it in your campaigns
- Meta may take some time to fully process the audience

This is normal and does not require action.

---

## Common questions

**Can I activate multiple audiences?**  
Yes, but for initial testing, we recommend activating one audience at a time.

**Is activation permanent?**  
No. You can stop using an audience at any time without affecting others.
