---
title: "Activate Your First Audience"
description: "How to choose an audience from the catalog and deliver it to your Meta account."
---

Activating an audience makes it available inside your Meta Ads account as a custom audience, ready to use in campaigns.

## How to activate

<Steps>

  <Step title="Go to Audiences">

      In your Slopeside dashboard, open the Audiences tab and click Browse Catalog.

        </Step>

          <Step title="Find your audience">

              Browse by category or use search to find an audience that matches your target buyer. Slopeside has 116 pre-built segments across DTC and B2B verticals.

                  Not sure which audience to choose? See [How to Choose the Right Audience](/user-guide/manage/catalog).

                    </Step>

                      <Step title="Activate">

                          Click Activate on the audience. Slopeside begins syncing it to your connected Meta Ads account.

                            </Step>

                              <Step title="Wait for delivery">

                                  Delivery typically completes within 24 hours. The audience status will update to Active in your dashboard when it's ready.

                                    </Step>

                                      <Step title="Find it in Meta">

                                          Once active, the audience appears in Meta Ads Manager under Audiences. It will be labeled with the Slopeside audience name. It is now ready to use in a campaign.

                                            </Step>

                                            </Steps>

                                            <Warning>

                                            Do not manually edit a synced Slopeside audience inside Meta Ads Manager. Editing the audience in Meta will break the sync and prevent daily refreshes from working. All audience management should happen through your Slopeside dashboard.

                                            </Warning>

                                            ## What the daily refresh means

                                            Every Slopeside audience updates automatically every 24 hours. New people who have entered active research mode for your category are added; people who are no longer showing buying behavior are removed. You do not need to do anything — this happens in the background.

                                            ## Minimum audience size

                                            Meta requires a minimum of 100 matched users to activate a custom audience for delivery. Most Slopeside audiences exceed this threshold significantly, but if your audience shows as too small to deliver in Meta, contact support.

                                            ## What to do next

                                            Your audience is live in Meta. The next step is setting up a test so you can measure performance.
