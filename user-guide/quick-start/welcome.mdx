## Welcome to Slopeside

Slopeside is an audience intelligence platform built to improve performance on Meta Ads.

As privacy changes and automated ad delivery have reshaped how Meta works, many traditional targeting methods have become less reliable. Advertisers often struggle with higher costs, slower learning, and unpredictable scaling.

<Info>
Slopeside helps by providing Meta with stronger audience signals built from real research and evaluation behavior, so campaigns can learn more efficiently and perform more consistently.
</Info>

---

## What Slopeside does

Slopeside builds high-intent audiences based on how people research, evaluate, and explore products or services online.

These audiences are delivered into Meta Ads, where they can be used directly or as seed audiences for expansion and lookalikes.

<Note>
Slopeside does not manage campaigns, budgets, or creative. Meta remains fully in control of ad delivery and optimization.
</Note>

---

## Who Slopeside is for

Slopeside is designed for teams actively running Meta Ads, including:

- Consumer brands focused on efficient prospecting
- B2B advertisers looking to reach decision-makers on Meta
- Agencies managing paid media for multiple clients

It works best for teams that already understand Meta Ads fundamentals and want better audience inputs to support testing and scaling.

---

## How teams typically use Slopeside

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Meta Ads">
    Link your Meta Ads account so audiences can be delivered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select or request an audience">
    Choose an audience from the Catalog or request a custom one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate the audience">
    Make the audience available inside Meta Ads.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run a clean test">
    Compare the Slopeside audience against an existing one using the same setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scale or iterate">
    Expand, refine, or test new audiences based on results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What success looks like

Successful Slopeside users typically see:

- Clearer and more reliable testing outcomes
- Faster and more predictable learning behavior in Meta
- Less reliance on broad or generic targeting
- Easier expansion into new audiences or markets

<Info>
Results vary by use case, but consistency and direction matter more than short-term spikes.
</Info>
