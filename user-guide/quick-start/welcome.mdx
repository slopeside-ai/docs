## Welcome to Slopeside

Slopeside helps advertisers improve performance on Meta Ads by using stronger audience signals.

Over the past few years, changes to privacy and ad delivery have made traditional targeting methods less reliable. Many advertisers now struggle with higher costs, slower learning, and unpredictable results.

Slopeside exists to solve this by building high-intent audiences based on real research and evaluation behavior, then delivering those audiences into Meta Ads as optimization inputs.

---

## What Slopeside does

Slopeside:
- Builds high-intent audiences based on real behavior
- Delivers those audiences into Meta Ads
- Helps Meta’s algorithm learn faster and more accurately

Slopeside does not replace Meta Ads or control campaign delivery. Meta still handles bidding, optimization, and ad delivery.

---

## Who Slopeside is for

Slopeside is designed for teams actively running Meta Ads, including:
- Consumer brands
- B2B advertisers
- Agencies managing multiple accounts

It works best when introduced early in campaign setup, before Meta begins learning.

---

## What success looks like

Most successful teams use Slopeside to:
- Test new audiences cleanly against existing ones
- Reduce wasted spend from weak targeting inputs
- Scale more predictably over time

You don’t need to change your creative or campaign structure to get started.
