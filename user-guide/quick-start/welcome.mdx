## What Slopeside does

Slopeside delivers behavioral intent audiences directly into your Meta Ads account. These audiences are built from real-time browsing and search activity — people who are actively researching products or services in your category right now.

You use them as custom audiences in Meta, either as standalone targeting or as suggestions for Advantage+ campaigns. Meta's AI takes it from there.

Slopeside does not manage your campaigns, budgets, or creative. It provides better starting data for Meta's optimization system to work with.

## Who it's for

Slopeside is built for performance marketers who are already running Meta Ads and want better audience inputs. That includes:

- DTC brands spending $10K or more per month on Meta, looking to reduce CAC without starting from scratch every time

- B2B companies struggling to reach decision-makers on a consumer platform, where business emails don't match Meta profiles

- Agencies managing multiple client accounts who need a scalable audience edge across verticals

It works best for teams that understand Meta Ads fundamentals and are ready to run a structured test.

## How it works

<Steps>

  <Step title="Connect your Meta account">

      Link your Meta Ads account so Slopeside can deliver audiences directly to it.

        </Step>

          <Step title="Choose an audience">

              Browse 116 pre-built audience segments across DTC and B2B verticals, or request a custom audience for your category.

                </Step>

                  <Step title="Activate">

                      One click activates the audience. It appears in your Meta Ads account within 24 hours, ready to use.

                        </Step>

                          <Step title="Run a test">

                              Compare your Slopeside audience against your current best-performing ad set. Keep everything else identical — same creative, same budget, same objective.

                                </Step>

                                  <Step title="Read your results">

                                      The Performance tab tracks both ad sets and shows you which one is delivering better CPA.

                                        </Step>

                                        </Steps>

                                        ## What good results look like

                                        Results vary by vertical, budget, and test setup. What consistent Slopeside users typically see:

                                        - Lower CPA compared to their existing control audience

                                        - Higher CPM on the Slopeside ad set (this is expected — you are reaching a more qualified audience, and that costs more per impression)

                                        - Faster exit from Meta's learning phase when the test is set up correctly

                                        <Note>

                                        Higher CPM is not a sign that something is wrong. It reflects the higher match quality of the audience. Judge performance by CPA, not CPM.

                                        </Note>

                                        ## Audiences refresh daily

                                        Every Slopeside audience updates every 24 hours with new people who have entered active research mode for your category. You never need to upload or manually refresh a list.
