## What activations are

An activation makes a Slopeside audience available inside your Meta Ads account.

Activation does not launch ads, change budgets, or modify campaigns. It simply delivers the audience so Meta can use it.

---

## What happens during activation

When an audience is activated:
- Slopeside sends the audience to Meta Ads
- Meta processes the audience
- The audience becomes selectable in campaigns

Processing time can vary and may take some time before the audience appears in Meta.

---

## Activation states

You may see different activation states in Slopeside. These indicate whether:
- The audience is queued
- The audience is processing
- The audience is available in Meta Ads

Seeing a delay here is normal and does not require action.

---

## Can I activate the same audience more than once?

An audience only needs to be activated once per Meta Ads account.

Once active, it can be reused across campaigns and ad sets without reactivation.

---

## Common questions

**Does activation spend money?**  
No. Activation alone does not spend budget.

**Can activation affect live campaigns?**  
No. Campaign behavior only changes if you manually apply the audience.

---

## What to do next

After activation, you can select the audience inside Meta Ads.
