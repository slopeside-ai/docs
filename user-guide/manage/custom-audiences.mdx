## Custom audience options

If you don’t see the audience you need in the Catalog, Slopeside offers two custom audience options.

Each option is designed for different use cases.

---

## Requested Catalog audiences

You can request a new audience idea for inclusion in the Catalog.

If approved:
- The audience becomes available to all Slopeside customers
- It follows standard refresh and usage behavior

This option is best for broadly useful audiences.

---

## Private custom audiences

For highly specific needs, you can request a private custom audience.

Private custom audiences:
- Are built specifically for your account
- Are not shared with other customers
- Can include granular criteria such as specific search terms or niche behaviors
- Are created in collaboration with Slopeside’s Customer Success team
- Incur an additional fee

This option is best for unique products, niche markets, or very specific targeting needs.

---

## How the request process works

1. Submit a request with details about your target audience  
2. Slopeside reviews the request  
3. You’re notified whether it will be:
   - Added to the Catalog, or
   - Built as a private custom audience  

Timelines and pricing vary based on complexity.

---

## Common questions

**Are private audiences visible to Meta?**  
Meta receives the audience like any other activation. No special labeling is applied.

**Can a private audience become shared later?**  
No. Private audiences remain private unless explicitly agreed otherwise.
