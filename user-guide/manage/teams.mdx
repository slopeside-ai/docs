## Managing team access

Slopeside allows multiple users to access the same account so teams can collaborate.

This is useful for internal teams, agencies, and partners.

---

## Adding team members

You can invite team members to your Slopeside account and assign access so they can:
- View audiences
- Manage activations
- Review Catalog options

Permissions may vary depending on your plan.

---

## Who should have access

Consider giving access to:
- Media buyers
- Growth marketers
- Agency teammates
- Stakeholders who review performance

Avoid sharing login credentials. Use team access instead.

---

## Common questions

**Can I remove a team member later?**  
Yes. Access can be updated or removed at any time.

**Does adding users affect billing?**  
Team access does not change billing unless otherwise stated in your plan.
