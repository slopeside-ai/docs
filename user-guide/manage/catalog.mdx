## The Slopeside Catalog

The Catalog contains audiences that are available for immediate use.

These audiences are designed to cover common industries, products, and use cases.

---

## How to browse the Catalog

Use the Catalog to:
- Explore available audiences
- Understand how audiences are categorized
- Identify audiences relevant to your goals

Catalog audiences are shared across Slopeside customers.

---

## How Catalog audiences are added

Catalog audiences may be:
- Created by Slopeside
- Requested by customers and approved for shared use

All Catalog audiences go through a review process before becoming available.

---

## What if I don’t see my audience?

If you don’t see an audience that fits your needs, you have two options:
- Request a new audience for potential inclusion in the Catalog
- Request a private custom audience built specifically for your account

Both options are covered in **Custom Audiences**.

---

## Common questions

**Are Catalog audiences generic?**  
No. Catalog audiences are built around specific intent signals, not broad interests.

**Can I request changes to a Catalog audience?**  
You can submit feedback or requests, but Catalog audiences are not customized per account.
