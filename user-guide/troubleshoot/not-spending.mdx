## Check these first

Ad delivery issues in Meta are almost always caused by campaign or ad set settings, not the audience itself. Before assuming the Slopeside audience is the problem, work through this checklist:

- Is the campaign active and not paused?

- Is the ad set active and not paused?

- Is the ad active and approved?

- Is the daily budget set above Meta's minimum (typically $1, but practically $10 or more for meaningful delivery)?

- Is the billing method on your Meta account valid and not declined?

- Is the ad set scheduled to run during current hours?

If all of these are confirmed and the ad set is still not spending, continue below.

## Audience too small

If your Slopeside audience has fewer than 100 matched users in Meta, the ad set cannot deliver. Check your audience size in Meta Ads Manager under Audiences.

If the audience shows as too small:

- Confirm the audience has been active for at least 24 hours (delivery requires a full sync cycle)

- Check whether geographic targeting in the ad set is narrowing the eligible audience significantly

- Contact support if the audience size appears correct in your Slopeside dashboard but small in Meta

## Ad set in learning phase with low budget

An ad set in Meta's learning phase with a very low daily budget may spend inconsistently — delivering on some days and not others as Meta explores delivery options. This is not a malfunction.

If daily budget is below $50, consider increasing it to give Meta enough room to deliver consistently. Each ad set needs roughly 50 conversions to exit learning phase. A very low budget at a typical CPA can mean weeks in learning with erratic delivery.

## Ad rejected or flagged

Check the ad status in Meta Ads Manager. If the ad is flagged or rejected, Meta will not deliver it regardless of audience or budget settings. Common rejection causes include landing page issues, prohibited content categories, or policy violations. Resolve the rejection in Meta directly — this is outside Slopeside's control.

## Advantage+ audience suggestion not set up correctly

If you are using Advantage+ and the Slopeside audience was not added correctly as a suggestion, Meta may deliver to a different population entirely. Confirm the following in your ad set settings:

- Advantage+ Shopping Campaign (ASC+) is turned on

- Your Slopeside custom audience appears under Suggest an audience

- Targeting is set to broad — no additional interest or demographic filters that could conflict
