## Common testing mistakes

Most testing issues come from changing too many variables or making decisions too quickly.

Avoiding these mistakes leads to clearer outcomes.

---

## Changing multiple variables

Changing creative, budget, and audience at the same time makes results impossible to interpret.

Always isolate the audience variable.

---

## Ending tests too early

Meta needs time to learn.

Ending a test after a short period can lead to incorrect conclusions.

---

## Overreacting to short-term swings

Daily performance fluctuations are normal, especially early on.

Look at trends, not individual data points.

---

## Testing too many audiences at once

Testing multiple audiences simultaneously can split budget and learning.

Start with one audience, then expand once you see clear signals.

---

## Common questions

**Can I “fix” a test mid-flight?**  
If you change core variables, the test is no longer valid.

**Should I pause a losing test immediately?**  
Unless performance is severely off, allow time for learning before stopping.
