## Slopeside for DTC brands

Slopeside helps DTC brands reach consumers who are actively researching or evaluating products, not just browsing casually.

Instead of relying on interests or broad targeting, Slopeside focuses on real intent signals that indicate buying consideration.

---

## When DTC brands use Slopeside

DTC teams typically use Slopeside when they want to:
- Improve prospecting efficiency
- Reduce wasted spend from broad audiences
- Scale beyond existing customer lists
- Launch new products or categories more predictably

Slopeside works well even if you have limited first-party customer data.

---

## How DTC brands use Slopeside

Most DTC brands use Slopeside audiences:
- Directly in prospecting campaigns
- As seed audiences for Meta’s expansion and lookalikes

This gives Meta clearer signals about who is likely to convert, which can improve learning and performance over time.

---

## Common questions

**Do I need to change my creative?**  
No. Slopeside works best when tested with your existing creative so the audience is the only variable.

**Is this retargeting?**  
No. Slopeside audiences are prospecting audiences based on intent signals, not site visitors.
