## Slopeside for B2B advertising

B2B advertising on Meta is challenging because business professionals typically use personal profiles and emails on social platforms.

This makes traditional job title targeting and lead list uploads unreliable.

Slopeside addresses this by connecting business research behavior to social identities, allowing more intentional B2B targeting on Meta.

---

## When B2B teams use Slopeside

B2B teams use Slopeside to:
- Reach decision-makers on Facebook and Instagram
- Improve match rates compared to standard list uploads
- Prospect beyond existing CRM data
- Test new industries, roles, or verticals

Slopeside is especially useful when LinkedIn Ads are too expensive or restrictive.

---

## How B2B teams use Slopeside

B2B teams typically:
- Activate Slopeside audiences into Meta Ads
- Use them directly or as seed audiences
- Run clean A/B tests against existing B2B audiences

This allows Meta to optimize toward business intent rather than inferred job titles.

---

## Common questions

**Is this the same as uploading a lead list?**  
No. Slopeside audiences are built from observed intent signals, not static lead files.

**Does this replace LinkedIn Ads?**  
No. Slopeside is often used as a complementary channel to reach business users on Meta.
