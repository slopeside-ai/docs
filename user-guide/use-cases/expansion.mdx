## Expanding into new markets or personas

Slopeside is often used to expand beyond existing audiences once a baseline has been established.

This can include new products, customer types, or geographies.

---

## When teams use Slopeside for expansion

Expansion use cases include:
- Launching new products
- Entering new verticals or industries
- Targeting new buyer personas
- Testing new geographic markets

Slopeside helps reduce guesswork by grounding expansion in intent signals.

---

## How expansion testing works

Teams typically:
- Create a new Slopeside audience aligned to the expansion goal
- Activate it into Meta Ads
- Test it against existing audiences
- Evaluate performance directionally before scaling

This approach helps avoid broad, unfocused expansion.

---

## Common questions

**Should I expand before my core audience is stable?**  
Expansion is most effective after establishing baseline performance.

**Do expansion audiences replace existing ones?**  
No. They are typically tested alongside existing audiences.
