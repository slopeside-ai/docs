## Accessing your invoices

All invoices are generated through Stripe, Slopeside's billing processor.

To download an invoice:

1. Go to Settings then Billing

2. Select View Invoices

3. Find the invoice you need and click Download PDF

Alternatively, you can access your full billing history directly through the Stripe customer portal, linked from the Billing page.

## Invoice format

Invoices include your company name, billing address, plan name, billing period, amount charged, and Stripe transaction ID. They are suitable for accounting and expense purposes.

## International customers and VAT

If your business is registered for VAT or GST and requires a tax invoice with your registration number, contact support at support@slopeside.ai with your tax registration details. We can update your billing profile so future invoices include the required information.

## Missing invoice

If an expected invoice does not appear in your billing history, check that the payment processed successfully. A failed payment will not generate an invoice. If the payment was successful and the invoice is still missing, contact support.
