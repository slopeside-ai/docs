## Changing your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your Slopeside plan as your needs change.

Plan changes allow you to adjust:
- The number of audiences
- Feature access
- Team usage limits

---

## How upgrades work

Upgrades typically:
- Take effect immediately
- Unlock additional capacity or features
- Are reflected in your billing automatically

Upgrading is common after successful testing.

---

## How downgrades work

Downgrades:
- May take effect at the end of the current billing period
- May limit the number of active audiences or features
- Do not delete your data

Specific downgrade behavior depends on your plan.

---

## Common questions

**Will upgrading affect live campaigns?**  
No. Plan changes do not alter campaigns or activations.

**Can I downgrade after testing?**  
Yes, as long as your usage fits within the downgraded plan limits.
