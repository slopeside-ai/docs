title: "Upgrade / Downgrade"
description: "How to change your Slopeside plan."
---

## Upgrading your plan

To upgrade:

1. Go to Settings then Billing

2. Select Manage Subscription

3. Choose your new plan and confirm

Upgrades take effect immediately. You will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing period, then the full new plan price at your next renewal date.

## Downgrading your plan

To downgrade:

1. Go to Settings then Billing

2. Select Manage Subscription

3. Choose a lower plan and confirm

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Your current plan and audience access remain active until that date.

If your current number of active audiences exceeds the limit of the plan you are downgrading to, you will need to deactivate the excess audiences before the downgrade takes effect. You will receive a reminder before your billing period ends.

## Switching from trial to paid

Your $1 trial automatically ends after 14 days. To continue using Slopeside after your trial, go to Settings then Billing and select a plan before your trial expires. Your audience and any test data in the Performance tab will carry over.

If your trial expires without upgrading, audience syncing stops and your Meta custom audience will no longer refresh. You can reactivate at any time by selecting a plan.
