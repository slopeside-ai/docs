## Canceling your subscription

You can cancel your Slopeside subscription if it no longer fits your needs.

Cancellation behavior depends on your plan and billing terms.

---

## What happens when you cancel

After canceling:
- Access continues until the end of the billing period
- Existing audiences are no longer refreshed
- Activations stop being available for new campaigns

Your historical data is not immediately deleted.

---

## Before you cancel

Before canceling, consider:
- Whether you’ve completed a clean test
- Whether results need more time
- Whether a downgrade might be a better option

Many teams cancel after testing and return later.

---

## Common questions

**Can I reactivate later?**  
Yes. You can restart your subscription at any time.

**Does canceling affect Meta Ads?**  
No. Canceling Slopeside does not change your Meta campaigns.

---

If you have questions before canceling, check the **FAQ** or contact support.
