## Canceling your subscription

To cancel:

1. Go to Settings then Billing

2. Select Manage Subscription

3. Select Cancel Plan and confirm

Your access and audience syncing continue until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

<Warning>

Your audience data and Performance tab history are preserved for 30 days after your subscription ends. After 30 days, this data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. If you plan to return to Slopeside, reactivate before the 30-day window closes to retain your history.

</Warning>

## Pausing your subscription

If you need to stop using Slopeside temporarily, you can pause rather than cancel:

1. Go to Settings then Billing

2. Select Manage Subscription

3. Select Pause Plan and choose your pause duration

During a pause, audience syncing stops and your Meta custom audiences will no longer refresh. Your account, audience settings, and Performance tab data are preserved. Billing resumes when you unpause.

## Before you cancel

If performance did not meet your expectations during your test, contact support before canceling. Common issues — audience category mismatch, test setup errors, insufficient conversion volume — are often fixable. We would rather help you get a valid result than lose you to a test that was not set up correctly.

Reach us at support@slopeside.ai.
