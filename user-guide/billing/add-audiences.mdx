## Activating an audience

To add an audience to your account:

<Steps>

  <Step title="Go to Browse Catalog">

      In your dashboard, open the Audiences tab and select Browse Catalog.

        </Step>

          <Step title="Find the audience you want">

              Browse by category or use search. You can preview audience details before activating.

                </Step>

                  <Step title="Activate">

                      Click Activate. If you have an available slot on your current plan, the audience will begin syncing immediately.

                        </Step>

                        </Steps>

                        ## If you are at your plan limit

                        If all your audience slots are in use, you will be prompted to either deactivate an existing audience to free up a slot, or upgrade your plan to add more capacity.

                        Deactivating an audience stops its sync to Meta but does not delete it. You can reactivate it at any time.

                        ## Custom audiences

                        If you need an audience for a category not available in the standard catalog, contact support at support@slopeside.ai. Custom audience development is available on Scale and Enterprise plans.
