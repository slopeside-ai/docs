## Adding more audiences

As your use of Slopeside grows, you may want to add additional audiences to your account.

Adding audiences allows you to:
- Test multiple use cases
- Expand into new markets or personas
- Scale once performance is proven

---

## How adding audiences works

Additional audiences can be added based on your plan.

Once added:
- The new audience becomes available in your dashboard
- It can be activated into Meta Ads
- It follows standard refresh behavior

---

## When to add more audiences

Teams typically add audiences when:
- Initial tests show positive results
- They want to test a new use case
- They’re expanding into new products or regions

Adding too many audiences at once can make testing harder to interpret.

---

## Common questions

**Do additional audiences affect existing ones?**  
No. Adding audiences does not change or reset existing audiences.

**Can I remove an audience later?**  
You can stop using an audience at any time. Billing behavior depends on your plan.

---

## What to do next
