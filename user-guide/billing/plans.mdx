## Available plans

Slopeside offers four subscription tiers. All plans include access to the full audience catalog, daily 24-hour refreshes, Meta Ads integration, and the Performance tab.

| Plan | Price | Audiences included |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $500 / month | 1 active audience |
| Growth | $1,500 / month | Up to 5 active audiences |
| Scale | $3,000 / month | Up to 15 active audiences |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited + custom audiences |

A $1 trial is available for new accounts, giving you 14 days to run a test with one audience before committing to a paid plan.

## What counts as an active audience

An active audience is one that has been activated and is syncing to your Meta account. Audiences you have not activated, or that you have deactivated, do not count toward your plan limit.

You can swap which audiences are active at any time from your dashboard. Deactivating an audience stops its sync and frees up a slot for a different one.

## Enterprise plans

Enterprise plans include unlimited active audiences, custom audience development for categories not in the standard catalog, agency multi-client dashboard access, and priority support. Contact us to discuss enterprise pricing.
