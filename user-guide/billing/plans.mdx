## Plans and pricing overview

Slopeside pricing is based on access to audiences and how they’re used.

Plans are designed to be flexible so teams can start small, test effectively, and scale as needed.

Exact pricing and plan details are shown in your account.

---

## What plans include

Plans generally include:
- Access to the Slopeside Catalog
- The ability to activate audiences into Meta Ads
- Ongoing audience refreshes
- Team access based on plan level

Some features may vary depending on your plan.

---

## How to choose the right plan

Most customers start with a plan that allows:
- One or a small number of audiences
- Clean A/B testing
- Room to expand once results are clear

If you’re unsure which plan fits best, starting smaller and upgrading later is common.

---

## Common questions

**Is there a minimum commitment?**  
Plan terms and commitments are shown during signup and in your account.

**Can I change plans later?**  
Yes. Plans can be upgraded or downgraded as needed.
