## Data supply chain compliance

All data used in Slopeside audiences is sourced from partners who operate under GDPR and CCPA-compliant data practices. Slopeside's vendor agreements include:

- Explicit restrictions on data resale or redistribution

- Requirements that data partners supply only anonymized, aggregated behavioral signals

- Contractual obligations for ongoing compliance with applicable privacy regulations

Data partners are reviewed for compliance on an ongoing basis. Slopeside does not work with data sources that rely on cookie-based tracking as their primary signal collection mechanism.

## Meta Custom Audience compliance

Slopeside audiences are delivered as Meta custom audiences using Meta's standard API. All audiences are created and managed in accordance with Meta's Custom Audience Terms of Service, which require that audience data be collected with appropriate user consent and that data be hashed before upload.

Slopeside handles all hashing and delivery. You do not need to do anything additional to comply with Meta's audience terms when using Slopeside audiences.

## Enterprise and procurement requirements

If your organization requires a Data Processing Agreement (DPA), security questionnaire response, or additional compliance documentation, contact us at support@slopeside.ai.

We do not publish a standard DPA template here as requirements vary by organization. We will work with your legal or procurement team directly to provide the documentation you need.

<Note>

If your organization requires SOC 2 certification or other specific security certifications, please contact us before subscribing. We can discuss our current security posture and documentation in detail.

</Note>

## GDPR and international data transfers

If your business operates in the European Economic Area and you are using Slopeside to reach EU-based audiences, contact us before activating EU-targeted audiences. We can provide information about our data transfer mechanisms and applicable safeguards under GDPR Chapter V.
