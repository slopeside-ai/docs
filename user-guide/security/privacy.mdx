## What data Slopeside uses

Slopeside builds audiences from anonymized behavioral signals — browsing and search activity that indicates a person is actively researching a product or service category. These signals are collected and processed at the audience level.

Slopeside does not use:

- Personally identifiable information attached to behavioral records

- Your customers' data

- Data from your Meta campaigns to build audiences for other customers

- Cookies or device-level tracking

The identifiers used for Meta custom audience matching (hashed emails and phone numbers) are processed using the same SHA-256 hashing standard Meta requires for all custom audience uploads. Slopeside never exposes raw personal data to customers or to Meta outside of the standard hashed matching process.

## Regulatory compliance

Slopeside's data collection and processing practices are designed to comply with:

- GDPR — applicable to users in the European Economic Area

- CCPA — applicable to consumers in California

- Meta's Custom Audience Terms — required for all custom audience delivery on Meta's platform

## Opt-out

Individuals who do not wish to be included in behavioral intent audiences can opt out through standard privacy mechanisms, including browser-level privacy settings, opt-out registries, and direct requests to data processors. Slopeside honors opt-out signals received through its data supply chain.

## Your data as a customer

Slopeside accesses your Meta Ads account for two purposes only:

1. To deliver custom audiences to your connected ad account

2. To pull ad set-level performance data for display in your Performance tab

Slopeside does not read, store, or use your campaign creative, targeting settings, budgets, or any other campaign data beyond what is required to display performance metrics in your dashboard.

For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](https://www.slopeside.ai/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://www.slopeside.ai/terms).
