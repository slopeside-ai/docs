## Built for modern Meta advertisers

Slopeside is designed for teams that rely on Meta Ads as a core growth channel and need better audience signals to scale efficiently.

It supports both consumer and business-focused use cases.

---

## Slopeside for DTC brands

DTC brands use Slopeside to identify consumers who are actively researching or evaluating products and categories.

Common goals include:

- Finding higher-intent buyers earlier in the funnel  
- Reducing wasted spend on broad or generic audiences  
- Improving performance when customer lists are limited  
- Scaling beyond existing customer data  

Slopeside helps Meta learn faster by providing clearer signals about purchase intent.

---

## Slopeside for B2B advertisers

B2B advertising on Meta is historically difficult due to poor match rates and unreliable job title targeting.

Slopeside is especially effective for B2B because it connects business research behavior to social identities, making it possible to intentionally reach business professionals on Meta platforms.

B2B teams use Slopeside to:

- Reach decision-makers on Instagram and Facebook  
- Improve match rates compared to traditional lead lists  
- Build scalable B2B seed audiences  
- Expand into new roles, industries, or markets  

---

## Teams that get the most value

Slopeside is commonly used by:

- Performance marketing teams  
- Growth marketers  
- Media buyers and agencies  
- Founders running paid acquisition  

It works best for teams that understand Meta Ads fundamentals and want better inputs for optimization.

---

## How teams typically use Slopeside

Most teams follow a repeatable pattern:

1. Identify a target audience or use case  
2. Build a high-intent Slopeside audience  
3. Deliver it into Meta Ads  
4. Monitor performance and learning  
5. Repeat for new products or personas  

This approach allows for both depth within existing demand and intentional expansion into new audiences.
