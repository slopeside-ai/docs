## What this section is for

The goal of Getting Started is simple: help you go from account setup to your first live audience on Meta Ads as cleanly and quickly as possible.

Slopeside works best when it’s set up correctly before campaigns begin learning. This section walks through the required steps in the right order and highlights common mistakes to avoid.

---

## What you’ll accomplish

By the end of this section, you will:

- Connect your Meta Ads account
- Confirm required permissions
- Understand where audiences live in Slopeside
- Be ready to activate your first audience into Meta Ads

You do not need to configure campaigns or ads yet. This is about setup, not optimization.

---

## How Slopeside fits into your workflow

Most teams introduce Slopeside early in their campaign workflow.

A typical setup looks like this:

1. Connect Meta Ads to Slopeside  
2. Build or select a high-intent audience  
3. Activate that audience into Meta Ads  
4. Launch or update campaigns using the Slopeside audience as a seed  
5. Allow Meta’s optimization system to learn  

Using Slopeside after campaigns are already live can still work, but results are more predictable when audiences are introduced before Meta begins learning.

---

## What you need before starting

Before moving forward, make sure you have:

- Admin access to your Meta Ads account
- Access to the Meta Business Manager that owns the ad account
- A basic understanding of your target audience or use case

If you don’t have admin access, you’ll need to coordinate with whoever manages your Meta account before continuing.
