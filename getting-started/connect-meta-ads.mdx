## Why this step matters

Slopeside delivers audiences directly into Meta Ads. To do that, it must be connected to the correct Meta account with the proper permissions.

Most setup issues come from incomplete access or connecting the wrong account. Taking a few minutes to verify this upfront prevents issues later.

---

## What gets connected

When you connect Meta Ads to Slopeside, you are authorizing Slopeside to:

- Deliver audiences into your Meta Ads account
- Update those audiences as they refresh
- Maintain audience availability for campaign use

Slopeside does not create or manage campaigns, ads, or budgets.

---

## Required permissions

The Meta user connecting the account must have:

- Admin access to the Meta Ads account
- Permission to manage custom audiences
- Access through the correct Business Manager

If the user does not have these permissions, the connection may appear successful but audiences may fail to activate later.

---

## Connecting your account

To connect Meta Ads:

1. Navigate to the Integrations or Settings area in Slopeside  
2. Select **Connect Meta Ads**  
3. Log in using the Meta account with admin access  
4. Confirm the correct ad account during authorization  

Once connected, Slopeside will display the connection status in your dashboard.

---

## Common connection issues

Some common issues include:

- Connecting with a personal Meta account instead of the business admin
- Selecting the wrong ad account during authorization
- Missing permissions for custom audiences
- Access being revoked later inside Meta Business Manager

If something doesn’t look right, do not proceed to activation yet. Fixing access issues early avoids wasted time.
