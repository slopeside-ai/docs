# Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What does Slopeside actually do?">
    Slopeside builds high-intent audiences based on real research and evaluation behavior, then delivers those audiences into Meta Ads.

    It helps Meta’s algorithm learn faster and target more effectively by providing stronger audience signals. Slopeside does not replace Meta Ads or manage campaigns for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Slopeside best for?">
    Slopeside is best for teams actively running Meta Ads, including DTC brands, B2B advertisers, and agencies.

    It works especially well when introduced early in campaign setup and when teams want a clean way to test and scale audiences.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is this different from interest targeting or job titles?">
    Interest targeting and job titles rely on inferred or self-reported attributes.

    Slopeside audiences are built from observed behavior that indicates real intent, which can provide clearer signals for Meta’s optimization system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where does Slopeside’s data come from?">
    Slopeside uses aggregated, privacy-compliant behavioral signals related to research and evaluation activity.

    It does not rely on scraping social profiles, selling personal data, or uploading third-party lead lists.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Slopeside compliant with privacy regulations?">
    Yes. Slopeside is built with privacy and compliance in mind.

    Audience data is aggregated and processed in accordance with applicable regulations such as GDPR and CCPA. Slopeside does not store or sell personally identifiable information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Slopeside work for B2B advertising?">
    Yes. Slopeside is especially effective for B2B use cases on Meta.

    It connects business research behavior to social identities, making it possible to reach business decision-makers on platforms like Facebook and Instagram where job title targeting is unreliable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this the same as uploading a lead list?">
    No. Slopeside does not upload static lead lists.

    Audiences are built from behavioral signals and refreshed over time, which allows Meta to optimize toward intent rather than fixed records.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How should I test Slopeside?">
    Test Slopeside using a controlled A/B test.

    Use the same campaign structure, creative, and budget, and change only the audience. This isolates the impact of Slopeside’s audience signals.

    See the Testing section for step-by-step guidance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to see results?">
    Results vary depending on use case, budget, and campaign setup.

    Like any Meta Ads test, Slopeside requires a learning period. Early fluctuations are normal, and performance should be evaluated over time rather than day-to-day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I don’t see the audience I need in the Catalog?">
    If you don’t see an audience that fits your needs, you can request a new audience.

    Some requests may be approved for inclusion in the shared Catalog, while others may be built as private custom audiences.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What’s the difference between shared and private custom audiences?">
    Shared custom audiences, if approved, become available to all Slopeside customers.

    Private custom audiences are built specifically for your account, are not shared with other customers, and are created in collaboration with Slopeside’s Customer Success team for an additional fee.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can you build very specific or niche audiences?">
    Yes. Private custom audiences can be built for highly specific use cases, including niche markets, unique products, or granular targeting requirements.

    These audiences are tailored to your needs and are not visible to other customers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Slopeside manage my Meta campaigns?">
    No. Slopeside does not manage campaigns, budgets, bids, or creative.

    It provides audiences only. You remain in full control of your Meta Ads setup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add team members to my account?">
    Yes. Slopeside supports team access so multiple users can collaborate within the same account.

    Team access and limits depend on your plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel my plan?">
    Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel based on your needs.

    Details about how changes take effect are available in the Billing section of the User Guide.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    If you cancel, access continues until the end of the billing period.

    Existing audiences stop refreshing, but canceling does not affect your Meta Ads campaigns.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    If you still have questions or need help, contact support through the Help & Support tab.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
