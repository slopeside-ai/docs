## The short version

Meta Ads works best when it has strong signals to learn from.

Over the past few years, changes to privacy, tracking, and ad delivery have made it harder for Meta to reliably understand who is most likely to convert. As a result, many advertisers see higher CPAs, slower learning, and less predictable scaling.

Slopeside exists to solve this specific problem.

Instead of trying to outsmart Meta’s algorithm, Slopeside **improves the quality of the audience signals Meta already uses**.

---

## Why Meta targeting changed

Meta’s ad ecosystem has shifted significantly since the introduction of privacy changes like iOS14.5 and the move toward more automated, AI-driven delivery.

These changes reduced the amount of user-level behavioral data available for targeting and attribution. Traditional strategies such as interest stacking, job title targeting, and narrow manual segmentation have become less reliable.

At the same time, Meta has increased its reliance on automated optimization systems that learn from performance signals over time.

The result is a simple truth:

> Meta still works. But it needs better inputs.

---

## What Slopeside does differently

Slopeside builds **high-intent audiences based on real research and evaluation behavior**, rather than static demographics or assumed interests.

These audiences act as **strong seed inputs** for Meta’s optimization system.

When you deliver a Slopeside audience into Meta Ads, you are giving Meta a clearer signal about who matters most. This helps Meta:

- Target users in the seed audience more effectively
- Find additional users with similar behavioral patterns
- Learn faster during the campaign learning phase
- Reduce wasted spend caused by weak or noisy targeting inputs

Slopeside does not replace Meta’s algorithm. It strengthens it.

---

## Seed audiences matter more than ever

Lookalike and algorithmic targeting on Meta start with a seed audience. The quality of that seed influences everything that follows.

In a post-privacy environment, advertisers often rely on:

- Smaller customer lists
- Incomplete pixel data
- Broad, generic audiences

These inputs limit how well Meta can optimize.

Slopeside helps restore signal strength by creating seed audiences that reflect **active intent**, not just past conversions or profile attributes.

---

## Supporting both B2B and DTC use cases

Slopeside supports both consumer and business targeting on Meta.

### DTC audiences

For consumer brands, Slopeside identifies users who are actively researching or evaluating relevant products or categories. These audiences help Meta find buyers earlier in the decision process and scale performance more predictably.

### B2B audiences

B2B targeting on Meta is historically difficult because business professionals often use personal emails and profiles on social platforms.

Slopeside addresses this by connecting business research behavior to social identities more effectively than standard list uploads. This allows advertisers to intentionally reach business decision-makers on platforms like Instagram and Facebook without relying on unreliable job title targeting.

---

## What Slopeside does not do

To set clear expectations, Slopeside does not:

- Override or bypass Meta’s algorithm
- Guarantee specific CPAs or conversion outcomes
- Replace creative testing or offer optimization
- Provide manual audience micromanagement

Slopeside focuses on one thing: **delivering stronger audience signals into Meta Ads**.

Most customers use Slopeside early in their campaign setup process to:

1. Build high-intent seed audiences

4. Repeat the process for new products, personas, or markets
