## What Slopeside is

Slopeside is an audience intelligence platform designed to improve performance on Meta Ads by strengthening audience signals.

It does this by building high-intent audiences based on real research and evaluation behavior, then delivering those audiences into Meta as seed inputs for optimization.

Slopeside is most effective when used early in campaign setup, before Meta begins learning and allocating budget.

---

## What Slopeside is not

Slopeside is not a replacement for Meta Ads, and it does not control how Meta delivers ads.

Specifically, Slopeside does not:

- Replace Meta’s optimization or delivery systems  
- Act as a bidding or budget management tool  
- Guarantee performance outcomes  
- Eliminate the need for strong creative and offers  

Slopeside strengthens inputs. Meta still controls delivery.

---

## What problem Slopeside solves

Many advertisers struggle with:

- Weak or noisy targeting inputs  
- Low match rates from customer or lead lists  
- Overreliance on interests or job titles  
- Unpredictable performance after privacy changes  

Slopeside addresses these challenges by helping Meta better understand who matters most, based on intent rather than assumptions.

---

## When Slopeside is a good fit

Slopeside is a strong fit if you:

- Are actively running Meta Ads  
- Want more predictable scaling  
- Need better seed audiences for optimization or lookalikes  
- Advertise to either consumers or business professionals  

Slopeside works best when paired with thoughtful campaign structure and strong creative.

---

## When Slopeside may not be the right tool

Slopeside may not be a fit if you:

- Are not running Meta Ads  
- Expect instant results without a learning phase  
- Rely solely on interest-based targeting  
- Are looking for a full-service ad management solution  

Understanding this upfront leads to better outcomes.
